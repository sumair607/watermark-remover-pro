Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our watermark removal service

What types of watermarks can you remove?

Our advanced algorithm can remove various types of watermarks including:

  • Text watermarks
  • Logo watermarks
  • Semi-transparent overlays
  • Corner watermarks
  • Red-colored watermarks (common in videos)
What file formats do you support?

We support both video and image files:

  • Videos: MP4, AVI, MOV, MKV
  • Images: JPG, JPEG, PNG, BMP

Maximum file size: 100MB

How long does processing take?

Processing time depends on file size and complexity:

  • Images: Usually 10-30 seconds
  • Short videos (under 1 minute): 1-3 minutes
  • Longer videos: 3-10 minutes

Larger files may take longer to process.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take your privacy seriously:

  • Files are automatically deleted after processing
  • No personal data is stored
  • Secure SSL encryption for all transfers
  • No third-party access to your files
Is this service really free?

Yes! Our basic watermark removal service is completely free. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will remain free for personal use.

What are the legal considerations?

Please use our service responsibly:

  • Only remove watermarks from content you own or have permission to modify
  • Respect copyright laws and intellectual property rights
  • Never use this service to infringe on others' rights
  • Read our full Legal Disclaimer
What if the watermark isn't completely removed?

While our algorithm is highly effective, some watermarks may be challenging to remove completely due to:

  • Complex backgrounds
  • Large or intricate watermarks
  • Low-quality source files
  • Watermarks that cover important details

For best results, use high-quality source files with clearly defined watermarks.
